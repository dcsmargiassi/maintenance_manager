Privacy Policy for Vehicle Record Tracker

Effective Date: June 28, 2025

Vehicle Record Tracker (“we”, “our”, or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use your information.

Information We Collect

Vehicle Record Tracker collects only limited personal information necessary for account creation and authentication. All vehicle and fuel data you enter is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted or stored externally.

We also collect anonymized analytics data to better understand app usage, which helps us improve the user experience.

Use of Firebase Firestore

We use Firebase Firestore to manage account creation and authentication data. Firebase may collect metadata such as device information and usage logs as part of its service operation. No vehicle or fuel data is stored in Firebase Firestore.

Data Usage

  • Account-related data is securely stored using Firebase Firestore.
  • Vehicle and fuel information remain on your device only.
  • No user data is shared with third-party advertisers or analytics services.

Use of Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized data about how users interact with our app. This includes information such as device type, app usage patterns, and approximate geographic location derived from your IP address. This data helps us understand user behavior and improve our app's performance and features.

Use of Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to help understand how users interact with our app. This includes data such as the type of device used, screen views, session duration, and general geographic region (based on IP address). This information is anonymized and aggregated to ensure individual users cannot be identified.

The purpose of collecting this data is to help us improve app functionality, identify popular features, and guide future updates like regional language support. Firebase Analytics does not access personal information like your name, email, or vehicle data.

You may choose to disable analytics tracking from the app’s settings page at any time.

Third-Party Services

Besides Firebase, we do not use other third-party analytics or advertising services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here.
