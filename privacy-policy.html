Privacy Policy for Maintenance Manager

Effective Date: June 28, 2025

Maintenance Manager (“we”, “our”, or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use your information.

Information We Collect

Maintenance Manager collects only limited personal information necessary for account creation and authentication. All vehicle and fuel data you enter is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted or stored externally.

Use of Firebase Firestore

We use Firebase Firestore to manage account creation and authentication data. Firebase may collect metadata such as device information and usage logs as part of its service operation. No vehicle or fuel data is stored in Firebase Firestore.

Data Usage

  • Account-related data is securely stored using Firebase Firestore.
  • Vehicle and fuel information remain on your device only.
  • No user data is shared with third-party advertisers or analytics services.

Third-Party Services

Besides Firebase, we do not use other third-party analytics or advertising services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here.
